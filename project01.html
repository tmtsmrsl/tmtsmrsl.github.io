The dashboard above shows life expectancy across all countries from the year 2000 to 2020.
Here are some notable insights from the visualization above:
- Life expectancy around the world keep increasing over the last 20 years.
- Hongkong, Japan, and Macao are always in the top 5 countries with the highest life expectancy over the last 20 years.
- North America region has the highest life expectancy, while Sub-Saharan Africa region has the lowest life expectancy.
- Life expectancy is strongly correlated with health expenditure per capita.
- Life expectancy is also strongly correlated with gross domestic product (GDP) per capita.
