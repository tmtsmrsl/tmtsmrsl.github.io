The project is hosted on Streamlit, presenting informations and visualizations regarding gender equality in Indonesia (inspired by Our World in Data). The project discuss some gender-related indicators sourced from Badan Pusat Statistik. The raw data (in form of Excel files), notebook for data cleaning and exploration steps, and source code of the Stramlit web app are available on my Github.
